PUBG

PUBG (PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds) and is an online multiplayer battle royale game whereplayers fight to be the last one. The game can be played solo, duo,or with a squad of up to four players. It was released for PC in 2017 and is also available for Xbox One, PS4 and mobile devices. The game is played from either a 3rd person or 1st person perspective. In the game, up to one hundred players parachute onto an island. It was first released for Windows Steam's early access beta program in March 2017, with a full release in December 2017.

VALORANT

Valorant takes inspiration from the Counter-Strike series, borrowing several mechanics such as the buy menu, spray patterns, and inaccuracy while moving. The player will get 5 unlocked agents when they create their account. VALORANT is a character-based 5v5 tactical shooter set on the global stage. Outwit, and outshine your competition with tactical abilities, precise gunplay, and adaptive teamwork. The game has an assortment of weapons including secondary guns like sidearms and primary guns like shotguns, machine guns etc.

FORTNITE

Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. It is free-to-play battle royale game in which up to 100 players fight to be the last person standing, a cooperative hybrid tower defense-shooter and survival game in which up to four players fight zombie-like creatures and defend objects with traps they can build, in which players are given complete freedom to create battle arenas. Battle Royale were released in 2017 as early access, while Creative was released on December 6 2018. Save the World also available for macOS.
